Report on the " Paint Museum "
In order to write / read this report , I spent hours at this wonderful museum .
There , I saw the exhibition and admired the building itself .
I walked around the garden too .
Location
The museum was located in Ouchy , in front of the " Delemuraz Hotel " .
It is near to the main road and within 300 meters of the station .
Outside
The building was made of white stones .
It had two floors .
Earlier , you had to pas through a huge garden .
There , ponds , pool , trees , flowers and ducks can be admired .
I recommended you take your swim suit on , because in the summer , you are allowed to swim in pool .
Outside
If you are inside the museum you will be impressed by the paintings on the streets .
They were all painted during the war by nurses .
If you take a few steps , you will discover a ceiling .
Everything in this room is about wine .
To learn the whole history of a building , there is a room dedicated to it on the first floor .
Particularly , I recommend you visit the " blue " room .
Nothing in this room is blue . The carpet , the ceiling , and even the door .
According to the owner , it was a room where people used to go to in the past when they were depressed .
My conclusion :
To sum up , I want to add that this house is the most popular in our city .
You would come and visit it for the pictures or for the building itself .
You ca n't be disappointed .
The only negative point is that you must pay 10 francs to visit the house and the garden .
